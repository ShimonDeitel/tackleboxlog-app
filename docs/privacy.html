Privacy Policy

Tackle Box Log is an offline-first app. All data you enter — items, notes, and any catch log entries — is stored locally on your device in the app's private storage. We do not collect, transmit, or sell any personal data. There are no analytics or tracking SDKs in this app.

Purchases are processed by Apple via StoreKit. We never see or store your payment details.

If you choose to attach a photo to a log entry, that photo also stays on your device and is never uploaded anywhere.
